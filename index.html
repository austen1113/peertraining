Adapted PE Ally Quest

A quick scenario game for peer support volunteers who help adults with disabilities during an afternoon adaptive PE program. Practice how to promote choice, dignity, safety, inclusion, communication, and fun.

Scenario choices Points + streak bonus Unlockable badges Coaching feedback

How to earn points

Pick the response that best supports the adult participant while protecting dignity and safety. Strong choices earn more points, build a streak, and unlock badges.

Best practices

Offer choices, use respectful language, support independence, model calmly, and adapt activities without taking over.

Try to avoid

Talking over the person, assuming helplessness, over-prompting, ignoring preferences, or correcting in a way that embarrasses them.

Quick coaching guide

Keep support person-centered. Start with respect, then offer choices, then fade help when the person can do more independently.

In adaptive PE, your goal is not to do the activity for someone. Your goal is to help them participate as fully, safely, and joyfully as possible.
